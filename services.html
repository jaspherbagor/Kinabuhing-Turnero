SERVICE CATEGORIES

WELDING

Welding is a process that involves joining two or more pieces of metal by melting them together using heat, pressure, or both. This creates a strong, permanent bond.

Welding is essential for constructing and repairing metal structures. It provides durability, structural integrity, and versatility.

MACHINING

Machining involves removing material from a workpiece to shape it into a desired form. This is achieved through cutting, drilling, milling, and other precision techniques using specialized machinery.

Machining ensures high precision and accuracy in metal components. It's crucial for creating custom parts with tight tolerances.

CUTTING

Cutting involves separating metal sheets or bars into specific shapes or sizes using various tools, such as saws, shears, or plasma cutters.

It allows for the creation of custom shapes and sizes, contributing to the versatility of metal in various applications.

FABRICATION

Fabrication involves assembling, bending, cutting, and shaping metal to create structures or components. This process often includes welding and machining.

Fabrication allows for the customization of metal components or structures to meet specific design requirements.

DRILLING

Drilling is the process of creating holes in metal using a drill bit. It can be done manually or with drilling machines.

Drilling is essential for creating holes needed for fasteners, pipes, and other components. Precise drilling ensures proper alignment and functionality in metal structures.

THREADING

Threading is the process of creating screw threads on the surface of a metal rod or pipe. This can be done using threading machines or manual tools.

Threading is vital for creating fasteners like bolts, nuts, and screws. Precise threading ensures secure connections, making it essential for industries where safety and reliability are paramount.
