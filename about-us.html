OUR JOURNEY

Since its establishment, Kinabuhing Turnero has been a beacon of excellence in the metal machining industry. Our journey is marked by a commitment to precision, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Milestones

Year 2022: Kinabuhing Turnero was founded with a vision to deliver unparalleled metal machining services to clients.

2023: Achieved ISO 9001:2023 certification, a testament to our dedication to quality and operational excellence.

Over the years, Kinabuhing Turnero has experienced consistent growth and expansion. What started as a modest workshop has evolved into a state-of-the-art facility equipped with the latest technology in metal machining.
